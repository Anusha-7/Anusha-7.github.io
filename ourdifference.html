OUR DIFFERENCE

Quality in everything we do, Quality in Optimizing Business, Operational, Workforce and Workflow Processes.

We are determined to demonstrate that maintaining quality in every single process is Economical, Profitable, Result Oriented and single measuring metric that balances all other equations.

Inc7 is committed to achieving and maintaining the highest levels of quality. Our goal is to be recognized as one of the world leaders in Customer Support Services. We deliver consistently high quality, reliable services on time, which meet or exceed our customer requirements. The QC Team ensures that the interactions of wizards with end-users meet the quality norms established by the company/client. The QC Team's primary responsibility is to randomly evaluate interactions on the basis of a set of evaluation criteria, which are primarily based on COPC Standards. The overall parameters of quality that the QC Team focuses on are:

1. Accuracy : To deliver accuracy in Reporting, Measurements, Data & any Media Content. To ensure that the customer receives what he wants accurately is a sure way to succeed. It is, therefore, paramount that every response addresses the customer needs. It contains factual accuracy and complete information, stated clearly and concisely.

2. Customer Service : Extending excellent customer service is the very core of our endeavours. Our effort is to establish a relationship of mutual respect, trust and confidence with our clients. Quality here would depend on courtesy, sincerity, you-attitude, and a helpful and friendly approach.

3. Processes : A robust functional business is a by-product of a quality process management. Applying the Production System methodology that is customized for the ITES, BPO industry; Inc7 is aggressive to deliver Quality Outputs by installing Quality Process Management Systems and Methodology.

4. People : Inc7 understands and knows that the most dynamic element of their business design is their people. Processes can be designed to be robust, Technology and Machines can be designed to be robust, however the most critical and dynamic element of the entire Business Construct are our people - Customers, Clients & Employees. Inc7 is determined to provide a 360° Quality Quality People Management Methodology and Processes.

5. Technology : We at Inc7 understands the seriousness and criticalities of providing the best in class technology to meet the needs of today's ITES, BPO industry; to be upbeat and have cutting edge, state of the art technology, to meet the needs of our Client, Customers and enable a balanced Economic Business, Mutually.

6. Language : An important criterion is the way information is communicated to the client. An effective use of language can convert even monotonous uninteresting information into exciting material. The components of quality, here would be grammatical and typographical correctness, to the point and precisely worded messages and an appropriate tone.
